About Us

We are a team of three dedicated graduate students who came together with a shared goal: to solve the challenges we personally faced when trying to book rooms for the clubs we are involved in. Frustrated by complicated and inefficient booking paper-based systems, we realized many others were struggling just like us. Additionally, hearing numerous stories about how difficult it is to navigate exam schedules motivated us to create a smarter, user-friendly solution. Our project aims to simplify room reservations and scheduling, making it easier for students and staff to plan their activities and exams smoothly. Driven by our own experiences and a passion for improving campus life, we built this platform to help everyone save time and reduce stress. We hope our work makes a positive impact on your daily routine.
